Activities & Travel

Whenever I am not working or in school I do my best to get out and see the world. These are some recent adventures I have gone one as well as a future vacation I cannot wait for.

Camping The Pinnacles

Went camping down south in the Pinnacles over Memorial Day weekend with some friends. The weather was great, we did a 12 mile hike, and we cooked some wonderful food over the campfire.

Monet & Gauguin @ the De Young Museum

My wife and I are art lovers and these sre two of our favorite artists. Monet's exhibit was focused on his late works, while Gauguins exhibit was about his love for Tahiti. highly recommend both!

Vacation to Michigan

Once Bootcamp is over my wife and I get to take a vacation to Michigan with my imediate family who all live in Chicago. It will be a much deserved vacation at that point.

Projects

Here are links to some of the projects I have worked regarding data analyzing

Atthowe Fine Art Services

Established in 1928 by John Atthowe as a barge company in the San Francisco Bay. This third generation company from the Atthowe family has evolved into something incredibly special. In 1975, grandson of founder John, Scott Atthowe switched the companies sole focus to fine art. Since he and Atthowe Fine Arts has solidified themselves as some of the best art installers in the world.

George Folz

George is an illustrator, cartoonist, and musician living in Evanston Illinois. Specializing in portraits of animals and people, while additionally working as a comic book artist. His 365 day celebration of Star Wars entitled #darthdays, was widely acclaimed. He collarborated with Eisner-award nominee Dan Goldman on Dandelion, and he partnered with late Seth Kushner from 2014-2015 to create The Roman Nose.

Green Bay Cycles

Opened in 2013 by Larry Faulkner and Sam Mack along the beautiful north shoreline of Lake Michigan in Illinois. This incredibly family friendly bike store is not only welcoming but full of knowledge and exoertise for all of your rding needs. Green Bay Cycles has been ranked among America's Best Bike Stores for a consecutive six years!

Ear Sense

earsense is the chamber music exploratorium built on a comprehensive database of composers, works and related multi-media resources including audio, video, program notes, scores, wikipedia pages and other links. Established in 1997 by Kai Christiansen who is a musicologist, multi-instrumentalist, radio host, writer, lecturer and general chamber music fanatic

72 & 10

Seventy Two and Ten is a band formed by George Folz and Sam Mack after childhood empowerment due to the exposure of what is still the best NBA season ever: the 1995-96 Chicago Bulls who went 72-10 during the regular season. Since forming into a cohesive group, Seventy Two and Ten have managed to bang out multiple albums that are easily available for all listeners.
