Hey there! My name is Peter Phelps Hardy.

I grew up in Chicago, Illinois and after spending five years in Oakland, California I have recently moved back to my hometown. I have been working in the art world as a fine art technician for over a decade and have been exposed to some amazing things. At the beginning of 2019 I began taking an interest in computer science and programming so I decided to enroll in a 6 month Data Analytics BootCamp at UC Berekeley Extension. The bootcamp took place during the evenings and weekends so I could continue to work full time at my current job to support my family. While gaining my data analytics certificate I learned how to implement and simply fell in love with Python, MySql, MongoDB, Flask, HTML/CSS, and Javascript to name just a few.

I am extremely excited to carve a new path in the technology sphere. I have a constant thirst for problem solving and building up my skills to become an expert in this field. Whether I am doing software or database development, I cannot wait for the opportunity to showcase my capabilities and demonstrate how far I have come in just a year. Since moving back to Chicago I have helped a small fine art handling company named Chicago Fine Art Packing & Crating build a website for a larger online presence. Currently I am working with a local realtor on her website for the same reason.

When I am not learning how to become better at programming and web design, I love spending my time creating art through drawing and printmaking. I also very much enjoy cooking with my wife & hanging out with daughter every chance I get. Moving back to Chicago has been such a breath of fresh air. I have felt so reenergized and ready to take on many new challenges. I have a lot of projects in the making that with my newly obtained skills are finally at the point of becoming tangible. Stay tuned for more and feel free to reach out at any time!
