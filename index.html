Hey there! My name is Peter Phelps Hardy.

I am originally from Chicago, Illinois, but currently live with my wife and new born daughter in Oakland, California. I have been working in the art world as a fine art technician for over a decade and have been exposed to some amazing things. Just this year I began taking an interest in computer science and programming so I decided to enroll in a 6 month Data Analytics BootCamp at UC Berekeley Extension. The bootcamp took place during the evenings and weekends so I could continue to work full time at my current job to support my family. While gaining my data analytics certificate I learned how to implement Python, MySql, MongoDB, Flask, HTML/CSS, and Javascript to name just a few.

I am extremely excited to begin a new career in the technology sphere. I have a constant thirst for problem solving and building up my skills to become an expert in this field. Whether I am doing software development or database administration, I cannot wait for the opportunity to showcase my capabilities and demonstrate how far I have come in just a year.

When I am not learning how to become better at programming, I love spending my time creating art, cooking & hanging out with my family as much as possible. Living in the Bay Area offers so many outdoor activities from camping in the redwoods to kayaking along the nearly infinite waterways. Since the weather is pretty much perfect all year round, my family and I take as much advantage as we can to enjoy it.
